Blender 3D Models
In GIMM 200, utilizing Blender, I created multiple 3D models. Two of the models were street objects, those being a cone and a barrel, both of which were colored, one of them also being textured. The third 3D model was an original spaceship design that was first designed in 2D, and then brought to life in Blender. Overall, I acquired some basic and advanced 3D modeling techniques that allowed me to create these models successfully and accurate to my vision.
